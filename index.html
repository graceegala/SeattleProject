Seattle: The Emerald City

Discover some of Seattle's most famous and scenic spots worth visiting.

Welcome to Seattle

Nestled in the Pacific Northwest, Seattle is famous for its vibrant culture, scenic views, and a perfect mix of city life and nature. From stunning parks to quirky landmarks, there’s something for everyone to enjoy.

Kerry Park

Located in Queen Anne, Kerry Park offers one of the most iconic panoramic views of Seattle’s skyline, with Mount Rainier in the background on clear days.

Learn more

Alki Beach

Alki Beach is a beautiful stretch of sand offering stunning views of downtown Seattle and the Olympic Mountains. It’s perfect for a day of sun and relaxation.

Learn more

Fremont Troll

Hidden under the Aurora Bridge, the Fremont Troll is a quirky, massive sculpture clutching a real Volkswagen Beetle. It’s one of Seattle’s most unique and beloved landmarks.

Learn more

Gas Works Park

Gas Works Park offers incredible views of Seattle’s skyline and Lake Union. It’s a favorite spot for picnics, kite flying, and watching the city lights during sunset.
